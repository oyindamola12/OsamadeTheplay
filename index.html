THE STAGE PLAY

Gold Lilies Global Services in collaboration with DeClassical Arts and Entertainment
Company presents a remarkable depiction of culture, women empowerment and
social justice in a phenomenal and musically infused stage play, Osamede!

OSAMEDE, meaning “Who the King has crowned” in the Benin language is a story
of an orphan girl, Osamede. Raised by her Uncle, she is chosen as a bride of the ruler
of the one of the largest provinces in Benin during a time of great economic development,
volatile political tension and severe oppression of her tribe. Facing the possibility of losing
her life and further endangering her people, she risks it all, leaning to courage over fear,
to change the lives of not just her community but that of generations to follow.

This intrigue filled and captivating stage play which is enacted through the ancient,
rich and classical Benin Kingdom with a thread of modernity, is an empowering body
of work and a strong call for our collective service to humanity that
will stir up hope and courage.

LILIAN OLUBI

LILIAN OLUBI Lilian Olubi is a Finance and investment professional with over
twenty years of Capital Markets experience. She is currently the CEO of a leading
Investment services firm in Nigeria and has had a diverse career in a few leading
institutions at different life cycles with a track record of driving growth. A woman
of many parts, Lilian is enthusiastic about public service and civic responsibility
and is a firm advocate for social enterprises and SMEs as the engine
house of a strong economy.

She founded The Gold Lilies Global Services, a social venture conglomerate providing value
across different sectors. She has been a boardroom judge for the past seven seasons of the successful
entrepreneurship reality TV show – ‘The Next Titan’ which promotes enterprise among youths by pruning
their business ideas, sharpening acumen and providing business capital. She sits on several boards
in the corporate and social sector and is the co-founder of Drug Aid Africa Initiative.

“Osamede, the Stage Play” which is her debut project in the arts is a tool she intends to utilize as an
avenue to raise the crucial topics of human rights, women’s role in social change, creation of job opportunities
for youths and to promote the creative arts industry in Nigeria with the strength of her corporate background.
Lilian is a Chartered Stockbroker and Authorized Dealing Clerk of the Nigerian Stock Exchange, she holds
a Bsc in Business Administration from the University of Benin and a Masters of leadership in
Development Finance from the Frankfurt school of Finance & Management, Germany.
She is also an author and is happily married and blessed with two lovely children.

AYO AJAYI

AYO AJAYI / Director / he is a theatre
producer and director is a pioneer music
director in the Nigerian theatre industry
who to date has directed well over 50 musicals
producing some of Nigeria's finests. His active
participation in the Nigerian theatre space
started when he directed the music of the
popular Saro the musical and Wakaa the
musical (Lagos & London) both from
the stables of BAP Production.

His musical talent was honed from a
very early age with lessons from his
father before been nurtured at the CMS
Grammar School where he eventually became
the School Choral director and pianist.
His debut was at the age of seven when
He performed at the Economic Community of
West African States (ECOWAS) conference
concert at the Sheraton Hotels in Abuja
with the group Rising Star led by Tosin
Adejumo and he has since then been
involved in several educational and
performance tours both in and
outside the country.

Ayo Ajayi is the Creative Director of
Declassical Arts & Entertainment, a top
production company in Nigeria which
has produced phenomenal musical theatre
performances like OMG the Musical, Legends
the Musical, The Lost Chord, Fela; Arrest
the Music, Gula’, Ajala, Home the Musical,
Ununcha the musical, to mention but a few.
He was also the Music Director for Midnight
Hotel, Wait, Evolution of African Music,
Full Circle, 'Obele and The Storyteller'.
Ayo, who has taught music in schools for
more than two decades is the immediate
past President of the MUSON School
Alumni Association and is also a
Pianist, Organist,Composer, and
Choral Conductor.
